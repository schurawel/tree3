Why MIT License?
---------------

We chose the MIT License for ResearchGuideUnearth for several important reasons:

Academic and Research Freedom
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

The MIT License is particularly well-suited for academic and research software because it:

* Allows the software to be freely used in both academic and commercial settings
* Enables researchers to modify the code to suit their specific research needs
* Facilitates sharing and collaboration across institutions

Maximum Adoption
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

The permissive nature of the MIT License encourages wider adoption:

* No restrictions on how the software can be used or extended
* Compatible with proprietary software, allowing integration into existing systems
* Reduces legal barriers to trying and using the software

Developer and Contributor Benefits
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

For developers and contributors, the MIT License provides:

* Clear attribution of the original work
* Protection from liability
* Simple terms that are easy to understand without legal expertise
* Compatibility with other open-source licenses

Long-Term Sustainability
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

The MIT License helps ensure the software remains useful over time:

* The community can continue to maintain and improve the software even if the original authors move on
* Commercial entities can invest in improvements with a clear understanding of their rights
* No license compatibility issues when integrating with other libraries and tools
