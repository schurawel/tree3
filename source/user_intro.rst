User Introduction
=================

Welcome to ResearchGuideUnearth! This section is designed to help you get started with using the application, understand its features, and make the most out of its capabilities for your research projects.

What you will find here:
------------------------

*   **Installation Guide**: Step-by-step instructions to install ResearchGuideUnearth on your system.
*   **Usage Guide**: Detailed explanations of how to use the core functionalities of the application.

We recommend starting with the :doc:`installation` guide, followed by the :doc:`usage` guide to familiarize yourself with the tool.
